Life expectancy at birth (years)

An interactive plot based on World Health Organisations (WHO) data: Indictor code: WHOSIS_000001

The plot shows the `life expectancy at birth` in years for each country in a given calendar year. You can:

  • Filter on sex (SEX_MLE - Male, SEX_FMLE - Female, SEX_BTSX - Both combined)
  • Select the year of interest using the slider
  • Click on a country to plot that countries specific data
